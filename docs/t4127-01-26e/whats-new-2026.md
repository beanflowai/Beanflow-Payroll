# What's New for January 1, 2026?

> Payroll Deductions Formulas - 122nd Edition

---

## Overview

This document outlines all changes to payroll deductions, tax rates, and formulas effective January 1, 2026. All updates are highlighted in grey boxes in the original PDF document.

## Indexing

For all federal and provincial or territorial indexed amounts for:
- Income tax
- Canada Pension Plan (CPP)
- Quebec Pension Plan (QPP)
- Employment Insurance (EI)

**Refer to Chapter 8** for complete indexed amounts.

Unless specified otherwise, indexing means that the values are adjusted based on changes to the Consumer Price Index (CPI).

## Federal Changes

### 2026 Federal Tax Rate Reduction

On May 27, 2025, the Government of Canada tabled a Notice of Ways and Means Motion with proposed legislation that reduces the lowest income tax rate from 15% to 14%.

**Transition Period (2025):**
- For 2025, the lowest tax rate was set at 14.5%
- Effective July 2025, a prorated tax rate of 14% was used

**2026 and Subsequent Years:**
- The lowest federal tax bracket will be 14%

### 2026 Federal Tax Rates and Brackets

| Income Bracket | Tax Rate | Constant (K) |
|:---------------|:---------|:-------------|
| Under $58,523 | **14%** | $0 |
| $58,523 to $117,045 | 20.5% | $3,804 |
| $117,045 to $181,440 | 26% | $10,241 |
| $181,440 to $258,482 | 29% | $15,685 |
| $258,482 and over | 33% | $26,024 |

### Federal Basic Personal Amount (2026)

The Federal Basic Personal Amount (BPAF) formula for 2026:

| Net Income (NI*) | BPAF Amount |
|:-----------------|:------------|
| NI* ≤ $181,440 | **$16,452** |
| $181,440 < NI* < $258,482 | $16,452 - [(NI* - $181,440) × ($1,623 / $77,042)] |
| NI* ≥ $258,482 | **$14,829** |

*(NI* = A + HD, where A = Annual taxable income, HD = Annual value of housing and board benefits)*

### Manitoba

**No Indexation for 2025 and Subsequent Years:**

The Manitoba Basic Personal Amount (BPAMB) and the Manitoba personal income tax bracket thresholds will not be indexed for 2025 and subsequent years.

**2026 Manitoba Tax Rates:**

| Income Bracket | Tax Rate |
|:---------------|:---------|
| Under $47,000 | **10.8%** |
| $47,000 to $100,000 | **12.75%** |
| $100,000 and over | **17.40%** |

**Manitoba Basic Personal Amount (BPAMB):**

| Net Income (NI*) | BPAMB Amount |
|:-----------------|:------------|
| NI* ≤ $200,000 | **$15,780** |
| $200,000 < NI* < $400,000 | $15,780 - [(NI* - $200,000) × ($15,780 / $200,000)] |
| NI* ≥ $400,000 | **$0** |

*(The maximum BPAMB for 2026 remains at $15,780)*
