## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
All employees are eligible immediately upon starting work.

Applies to the death of:
- Spouse (including common-law and same-sex)
- Child, step-child, foster child
- Parent, step-parent, foster parent
- Grandparent, grandchild
- Sibling, sibling-in-law
- Parent-in-law, child-in-law

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **2 days of unpaid leave** per calendar year following the death of a family member.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Ontario's 2-day entitlement is among the lowest in Canada (compared to 3 days in BC/AB and 5 days in MB/SK).
