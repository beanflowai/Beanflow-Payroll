## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- **Immediate Family**: 1 day **paid** + 2 days **unpaid**.
- **Extended Family**: Up to 3 consecutive days **unpaid**.
- **Pregnancy Loss**: Included in bereavement coverage.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Average of the daily wages for the previous 20 shifts.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
PEI is unique in the Atlantic region for mandating 1 paid day of bereavement leave for immediate family members.
