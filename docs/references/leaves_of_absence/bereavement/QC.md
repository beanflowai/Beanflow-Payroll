## Official Source
- [CNESST - Absence for death or funeral](https://www.cnesst.gouv.qc.ca/en/working-conditions/leaves-and-absences/absence-death-or-funeral)

## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
- **Paid Days**: Requires 3 months of continuous service.
- **Unpaid Days**: Available immediately.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- **Spouse, Child, Parent, Sibling**: 5 consecutive days, **2 of which are paid**.
- **Grandparent, Grandchild, In-laws**: 1 day unpaid.
- **Suicide/Criminal Offense**: Up to 104 weeks unpaid for death of spouse/child.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Average of the daily wages earned during the 20 working days preceding the leave.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Quebec is one of the few provinces that mandates paid days for bereavement (2 days), whereas most provinces (BC, AB, ON) are entirely unpaid.
