|-------------|---------|
| **Legislation** | NL Labour Standards Act, Section 43.12 |

| **Notice Period** | As soon as possible |
| **Documentation** | Not specified |

## Official Source
- [NL Government - Bereavement Leave](https://www.gov.nl.ca/ecc/labour/lsaissues/#bereavement)

## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
- **Paid Day**: Requires 30 days of service.
- **Unpaid Days**: 2 days unpaid are available after 30 days service; if less than 30 days service, 2 unpaid days are still available but the paid day is not. (Wait, search said: 1 paid + 2 unpaid after 30 days; 2 unpaid if <30 days).

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- **3 days total** (1 paid, 2 unpaid) for the death of an immediate family member.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Wage for the hours the employee would have worked.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
NL is one of the few provinces (along with PEI and QC) to mandate a paid day for bereavement.
