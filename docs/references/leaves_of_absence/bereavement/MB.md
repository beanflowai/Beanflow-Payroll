## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Employees are eligible after **30 days** of employment. Covers immediate family and pregnancy loss.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **5 days of unpaid leave** for the death of a family member.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Manitoba's 5-day unpaid entitlement is stronger than BC/AB (3 days) and ON (2 days).
