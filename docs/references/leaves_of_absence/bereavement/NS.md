## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **3 consecutive unpaid days** off work following the death of a family member.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Standard 3-day unpaid entitlement, same as BC and AB.
