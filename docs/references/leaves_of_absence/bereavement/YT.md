## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **1 week of unpaid leave** for the death of a family member.
- Special provision for **Funeral Potlatch**: Up to 1 week unpaid if designated to organize.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Yukon's "1 week" entitlement is unique compared to the "3 days" or "5 days" found in most provinces.
