## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
- **Unpaid Leave**: Immediate.
- **Paid Leave**: 3 months continuous service.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **10 days total** following the death of an immediate family member.
- The **first 3 days are paid**.
- **Child Death**: Up to 8 weeks unpaid (standard) or 104 weeks (criminal/suicide causes).

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Regular rate of wages.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
The federal government provides the highest number of days (10 total, 3 paid), significantly more than Ontario (2 unpaid) or Alberta (3 unpaid).
