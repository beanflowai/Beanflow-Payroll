## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **5 consecutive unpaid days** off work following the death of a close family member.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
New Brunswick's 5-day entitlement is more generous than BC (3 days) or Ontario (2 days).
