## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
Northwest Territories provides **Family Responsibility Leave** which covers bereavement.
- Employees can take up to **5 days of unpaid leave** per year for family-related responsibilities, which includes the death or funeral of a family member.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
NT's approach of merging bereavement into a broader "Family Responsibility Leave" is unique but provides more flexibility than Ontario's 2-day bereavement leave.
