## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
Similar to NT, Nunavut uses **Family Responsibility Leave** to cover bereavement.
- Employees can take up to **5 days of unpaid leave** per year for the death or funeral of a family member.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Follows the same model as the Northwest Territories.
