## Province-Specific Notes
- **Bereavement**: Yukon offers up to 1 week of unpaid leave for the death of a family member.
- **Family Violence**: 5 paid days + 5 unpaid days + 15 weeks unpaid.
- **Sick Leave**: 12 days unpaid sick leave is provided after immediate eligibility.
