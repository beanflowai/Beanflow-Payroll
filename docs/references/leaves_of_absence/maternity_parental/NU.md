## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **12 consecutive months**.
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents and adoptive parents.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 17 weeks. Can start 11 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**: Up to 37 weeks. Must start after maternity leave or upon birth/adoption, and end no later than 52 weeks after birth.
- **Combined**: Total **52 weeks**. (Note: Nunavut legislation has not yet been updated to match the 78-week extensions seen in other jurisdictions).

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Notice to Change**: 4 weeks' notice.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for maternity).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Nunavut's 52-week combined limit is now significanty shorter than the 78-week standard in most of Canada (BC, AB, ON, etc.).
- 12-month eligibility matches Yukon and NT.
