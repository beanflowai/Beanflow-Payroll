## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **20 weeks in the 52 weeks** immediately preceding the leave.
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents and adoptive parents.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 17 weeks. Can start up to 11 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**: Up to 62 weeks. Must be used within 78 weeks of birth/adoption.
- **Combined**: Total **78 weeks**.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Notice to Change**: 4 weeks' notice.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for maternity).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- PEI's eligibility (20 weeks in 52) is more specific than the 90-day/13-week standard.
- Combined 78 weeks aligns with AB/ON/BC.
