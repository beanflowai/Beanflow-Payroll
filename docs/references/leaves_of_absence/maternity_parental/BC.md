## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum length of employment is required for either leave.
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents, adoptive parents, or persons in a relationship of some permanence with the parent of a child.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 17 weeks. Can start up to 13 weeks before birth. Must continue for at least 6 weeks after birth.
- **Parental**:
    - Birth mothers: Up to 61 weeks (must start immediately after maternity leave).
    - Other parents: Up to 62 weeks (must start within 78 weeks of birth/adoption).
- **Extension**:
    - If maternity leave ends but birth hasn't occurred: Up to 6 weeks extension.
    - Child condition (physical/psych): Up to 5 additional weeks.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid leave.
- Benefits (Medical, Dental, Pension, Life Insurance) must be maintained if the employee continues to pay their portion.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks written notice before start.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request a medical certificate (pregnancy) or proof of adoption.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- BC is one of the few provinces with **no minimum service requirement** for maternity/parental leave.
- Combined duration (78 weeks) aligns with Federal EI Extended benefits.
