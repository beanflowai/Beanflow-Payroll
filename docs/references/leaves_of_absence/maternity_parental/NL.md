## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **20 consecutive weeks**.
- **Pregnancy**: Pregnant employees.
- **Child Care**: Natural parents, adoptive parents, or persons in a relationship of some permanence with a parent of a child.

### Entitlement
- **Pregnancy**: 17 weeks. Can start 17 weeks before birth.
- **Child Care**: Up to 61 weeks. Must be used within 96 weeks of birth/adoption.
- **Combined**: Total **78 weeks**.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 2 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for pregnancy) or proof of adoption.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
