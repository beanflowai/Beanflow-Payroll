## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum length of service** is required.
- **Pregnancy**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents and adoptive parents.

### Entitlement
- **Pregnancy**: 16 weeks. Can start 16 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**:
    - Up to 61 weeks for employees who took pregnancy leave.
    - Up to 77 weeks for employees who did not take pregnancy leave.
- **Combined**: Total **77 weeks**.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Notice to Change**: 4 weeks' notice.
- **Notice to Return**: 4 weeks' notice.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for pregnancy).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Notice requirement (4 weeks) is typical.
- 77-week combined duration is slightly shorter than MB (80) and QC (83) but aligns with AB/ON.
- No minimum service requirement is a major advantage.
