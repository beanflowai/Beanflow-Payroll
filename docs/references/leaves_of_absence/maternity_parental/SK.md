## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for more than **13 consecutive weeks**.
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents, adoptive parents, or persons in a relationship of some permanence with the parent of a child.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 19 weeks. Can start up to 13 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**: Up to 59 weeks. If taken with maternity leave, must start immediately after.
- **Combined**: Total 78 weeks.
- **Adoption**: 19 weeks of adoption leave, plus parental leave.
- **Pregnancy Loss (New 2026)**: Employees who experience a pregnancy loss up to 20 weeks before the due date are eligible for maternity leave.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.
- Employers must continue benefit plans if employees continue their contributions.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for pregnancy) or proof of adoption.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- SK's maternity duration (19 weeks) is slightly longer than the standard 15-17 weeks in other provinces.
- 13-week eligibility aligns with Ontario.
