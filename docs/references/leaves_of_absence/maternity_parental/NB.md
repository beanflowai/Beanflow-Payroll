## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum length of service** required.
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Child Care**: Natural parents and adoptive parents.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 17 weeks. Can start 13 weeks before birth.
- **Child Care**: Up to 62 weeks. Must be used within 78 weeks of birth/adoption.
- **Combined**: Total **78 weeks**.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**:
    - Maternity: Employee must advise employer 4 months before due date (or as soon as confirmed) and provide a medical certificate. Firm written notice of start date is required 2 weeks before.
    - Child Care: 2 weeks' written notice.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for maternity).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Notice requirement for maternity (4 months advisory) is unique to NB.
- 78-week combined duration is standard.
