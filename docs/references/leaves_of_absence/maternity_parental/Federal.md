## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum length of service** required for job-protected leave (effective since recent updates).
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents and adoptive parents.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 17 weeks. Can start up to 13 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**:
    - **Standard**: Up to 37 weeks (aggregate 40 weeks if shared).
    - **Extended**: Up to 63 weeks (aggregate 69 weeks if shared).
- **Combined**: Total **86 weeks** (if extended shared) or **71 weeks** (standard).

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: 4 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for maternity).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Federal standards often lead provincial updates (e.g., the 78-86 week extended options).
- No minimum service requirement is consistent with modern BC/NB/NS standards.
