## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum length of service** is required.
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents and adoptive parents.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 18 weeks. Can start 16 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**: Up to 65 weeks. Must start after maternity leave or upon birth/adoption, and end no later than 78 weeks after birth.
- **Combined**: Total **83 weeks**.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid by employer.
- Quebec has its own **QPIP (Quebec Parental Insurance Plan)** instead of federal EI.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 3 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Notice to Return**: 3 weeks' notice.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for maternity).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Quebec's maternity/parental system is distinct from the rest of Canada due to QPIP.
- Combined duration (83 weeks) is the most generous in Canada.
- No minimum service requirement is a major advantage.
