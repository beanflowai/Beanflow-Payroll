## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for at least **13 weeks** before the expected due date (for pregnancy leave) or before the start of the leave (for parental leave).
- **Pregnancy**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Birth parents, adoptive parents, and persons in a relationship of some permanence with a parent of a child.

### Entitlement
- **Pregnancy**: 17 weeks. Can start 17 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**:
    - 61 weeks for employees who took pregnancy leave.
    - 63 weeks for employees who did not take pregnancy leave.
- **Combined**: Total **78 weeks**.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 2 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Notice to Change**: 2 weeks' notice to change the start date or end date.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request a medical certificate (pregnancy).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Notice requirement (2 weeks) is shorter than BC/AB (4-6 weeks).
- 13-week eligibility is standard (matching SK).
