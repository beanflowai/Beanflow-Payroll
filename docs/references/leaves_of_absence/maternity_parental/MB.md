## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **7 consecutive months**.
- **Maternity**: Pregnant employees.
- **Parental**: Natural parents and adoptive parents.

### Entitlement
- **Maternity**: 17 weeks. Can start 17 weeks before birth.
- **Parental**: Up to 63 weeks. Must be completed within 18 months (78 weeks) of birth/adoption.
- **Combined**: Total **80 weeks** (one of the longest in Canada).

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate (for maternity).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Manitoba's 7-month eligibility is much longer than the 90-day/13-week standard in AB/ON/SK.
- Combined 80 weeks is slightly more than the 78 weeks in BC/AB/ON.
