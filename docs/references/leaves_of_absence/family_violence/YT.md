## Official Source
- [Yukon Government - Domestic or Sexualized Violence Leave](https://yukon.ca/en/employment/employment-standards/leaves-absence#domestic-sexualized-violence)

## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Employees with at least **3 months** of employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- **Up to 10 days**: The **first 5 days are paid**.
- **Up to 15 weeks**: An additional continuous period of unpaid leave.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Average day's pay.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Yukon's 5 paid days align with BC, ON, SK, and Federal standards.
