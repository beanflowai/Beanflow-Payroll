## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Employees are eligible after **90 days** of employment.

The leave applies if the employee or their dependent is a victim of interpersonal violence (domestic or sexual).

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
1. **10 Days total**: 5 days **paid** and 5 days **unpaid** (intermittent or consecutive).
2. **17 Weeks total**: A continuous period of unpaid leave.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Wage for the hours the employee would have worked.

### 4. Notice & Documentation Requirements
As soon as possible. Employers may ask for documentation but must maintain strict confidentiality.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Manitoba's policy is very similar to Saskatchewan's, providing 5 days of paid leave. It is significantly more generous than Alberta or BC (regarding the total 17-week extension).
