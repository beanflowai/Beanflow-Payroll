## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Employees are eligible after **13 weeks** of consecutive employment.

Applies if the employee or their child experiences or is threatened with domestic or sexual violence.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
1. **Up to 10 Days**: Taken individually or as a block. The **first 5 days are paid**.
2. **Up to 15 Weeks**: Taken as a block or in increments with employer consent. (Unpaid).

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Wage for the hours the employee would have worked.

### 4. Notice & Documentation Requirements
As soon as possible. Employers can request reasonable evidence.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Ontario's 13-week service requirement is longer than BC's immediate eligibility but standard for many other Ontario leaves. The 5 days of paid leave is consistent with BC and Federal standards.
