## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- **Up to 10 days**: The **first 5 days are paid**.
- **Up to 15 weeks**: An additional continuous period of unpaid leave.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Wage for the hours the employee would have worked.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
NT's policy is very progressive, matching the federal 5-day paid standard and providing immediate eligibility.
