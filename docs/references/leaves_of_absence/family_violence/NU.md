## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **10 days of unpaid leave** each year.
- Job-protected.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Nunavut currently only mandates unpaid days for family violence, unlike most other jurisdictions which offer at least 3-5 paid days.
