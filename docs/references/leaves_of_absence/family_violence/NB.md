## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Employees are eligible after **90 days** of employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
1. **Up to 10 days**: The **first 5 days are paid** (per calendar year).
2. **Up to 16 weeks**: A further continuous period of unpaid leave.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Wage for the hours the employee would have worked.

### 4. Notice & Documentation Requirements
As soon as possible. Employers may ask for reasonable documentation.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
NB's 5 paid days align with federal and BC/ON/SK/MB standards. The 16-week extension is one week shorter than Manitoba's but standard for the region.
