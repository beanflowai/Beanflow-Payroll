## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Employees with at least **3 months** of continuous employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **3 days paid** and **7 days unpaid** per 12-month period.
- Applies if the employee or their child experiences violence.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Average day's pay.

### 4. Notice & Documentation Requirements
Same as other provinces; reasonable notice and evidence.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
PEI's 3 paid days is less than the 5 days in NB/NS/ON, but higher than the 0 in AB.
