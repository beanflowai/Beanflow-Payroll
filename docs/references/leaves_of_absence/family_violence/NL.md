## Official Source
- [NL Government - Family Violence Leave](https://www.gov.nl.ca/ecc/files/labour-labour-standards-pdf-family-violence-leave.pdf)

## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available after **30 days** of employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **3 days paid** and **7 days unpaid** per year.
- Applies if the employee or their child experiences family violence.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Average day's pay.

### 4. Notice & Documentation Requirements
Notice and evidence requirements are standard.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
NL's 3 paid days is the same as PEI and Nova Scotia.
