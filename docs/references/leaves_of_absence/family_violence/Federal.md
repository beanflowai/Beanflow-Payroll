## Official Source

## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
- **Unpaid Leave**: Available immediately upon employment.
- **Paid Leave**: Requires **3 months** of continuous employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
- Up to **10 days total** in a calendar year.
- The **first 5 days are paid** if the employee has 3 months of service.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Regular rate of wages for their normal hours of work.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
The federal standard serves as the model for many provinces (BC, ON, NB, SK, MB).
