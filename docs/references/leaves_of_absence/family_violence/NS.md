## Detailed Rules

### 1. Eligibility
Available immediately upon employment.

### 2. Entitlement & Duration
1. **Up to 10 days**: The **first 3 days are paid**.
2. **Up to 15 weeks**: An additional continuous period of unpaid leave.

### 3. Payment Calculation
- **Rate**: Average of the daily wages for the previous 3 months.

### 4. Notice & Documentation Requirements
As soon as possible. Employers may ask for reasonable documentation.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Nova Scotia's 3 paid days is less than the 5 days found in BC, Federal, NB, and SK, but more than Alberta (0 days).
