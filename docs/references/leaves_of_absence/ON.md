## Province-Specific Notes
- **Serious Illness Leave (NEW)**: Introduced in late 2024/2025, providing 27 weeks of unpaid leave for serious medical conditions.
- **Bereavement**: Ontario only mandates 2 days of unpaid leave, which is lower than most other provinces.
- **Family Violence**: 5 days paid + 5 days unpaid + 15 weeks unpaid extension.
