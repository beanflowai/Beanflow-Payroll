## Province-Specific Notes
- **Recent Change (Nov 2025)**: Serious Illness or Injury Leave (49.4) was introduced, providing 27 weeks of unpaid leave for personal illness.
- BC is one of the more generous provinces regarding paid Family Violence leave.
- Bereavement leave is unpaid but applies to *each* instance of death.
