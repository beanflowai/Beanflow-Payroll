## Province-Specific Notes
- **Family Violence**: Nova Scotia offers 3 days of paid leave.
- **Parental Leave**: One of the longest in Canada at 77 weeks (when combined with maternity).
- **Bereavement**: 3 unpaid days for immediate family.
