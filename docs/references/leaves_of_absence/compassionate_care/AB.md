## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **90 days**.
- Applies to caring for a family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling, etc.).

### Entitlement
- 27 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave within a 52-week period.
- Can be shared by siblings/family members working for different employers.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 2 weeks' written notice before starting leaf (or as soon as possible).
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate from a physician or nurse practitioner.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Duration (27 weeks) is consistent with BC and Federal standards.
- 90-day eligibility is a standard Alberta requirement for most job-protected leaves.
