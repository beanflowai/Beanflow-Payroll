## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum service requirement**.
- Applies to caring for a family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling, etc.).

### Entitlement
- 28 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave within a 52-week period.
- Must care for a person who has a serious medical condition with a significant risk of death within 26 weeks.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer as soon as possible.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate required.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Standard 28-week duration.
- Yukon is one of the jurisdictions with no minimum service requirement for this leave.
