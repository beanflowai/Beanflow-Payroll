## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for more than **13 consecutive weeks**.
- Family member defined broadly (includes anyone the employee considers "like family").

### Entitlement
- 28 weeks within a 52-week period.
- Must be used to care for a family member who has a serious medical condition and is at significant risk of death within 26 weeks.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Employee must notify employer as soon as possible.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate required if requested by employer.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 28-week duration is standard across most provinces and aligns with Federal EI.
