## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **90 days**.
- Family member defined broadly.

### Entitlement
- 28 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave within a 52-week period.
- Must be used to care for a family member who has a serious medical condition and is at significant risk of death within 26 weeks.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must give notice of at least one pay period (unless impossible).
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate required.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 28-week duration is standard across most provinces.
