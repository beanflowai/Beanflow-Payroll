## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum service requirement**.
- Applies to caring for a family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling, etc.).

### Entitlement
- 27 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave within a 52-week period. (Wait, check if 27 or 28). Standardizing to 27 as per Act.
- Must care for a person who has a serious medical condition with a significant risk of death within 26 weeks.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer in writing as soon as practicable.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate required.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Standard 27-28 week duration.
- NT specifies 27 weeks in the Act (Sec 31.1).
