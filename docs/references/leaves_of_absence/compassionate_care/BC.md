## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum employment period required.
- Applies to "family members" (broadly defined, includes spouse, child, parent, sibling, grandparent, and "like-family" members).

### Entitlement
- 27 weeks within a 52-week period.
- Must be to care for a family member who has a serious medical condition with a significant risk of death within 26 weeks.
- If the family member does not die within 52 weeks, a new leave can start with a new certificate.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.
- Employers must allow employees to continue benefit contributions.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer as soon as possible.
- **Documentation**: A medical certificate stating that the family member has a serious medical condition and is at significant risk of death within 26 weeks.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Aligned with Federal EI duration (26-28 weeks).
- No service requirement is a key BC-specific advantage.
