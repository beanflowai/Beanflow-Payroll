## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum service requirement**.
- Applies to caring for a family member (spouse, child, parent, sibling, etc. - very broad definition).

### Entitlement
- 28 weeks of unpaid, job-protected leave within a 52-week period.
- Can be shared among siblings/family members.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer in writing (if possible) as soon as practicable.
- **Documentation**: Medical certificate required (stating the family member has a serious medical condition with a significant risk of death within 26 weeks).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Ontario refers to this as "Family Medical Leave", whereas most provinces use "Compassionate Care Leaf".
- 28-week duration is standard.
