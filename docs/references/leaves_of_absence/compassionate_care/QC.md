## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum service requirement** for the unpaid leave.
- **Paid Days**: 2 days paid after 3 months of continuous service (can be used for this or sickness).

### Entitlement
- **Standard**: 26 weeks in a 12-month period to care for a relative or person for whom the employee acts as a caregiver.
- **Minor Child**: Up to 104 weeks if the child has a serious potentially fatal illness or injury.
- **Criminal Act**: Up to 104 weeks if the relative's injury/death results from a criminal act.

### Payment Calculation
- First 2 days of sickness or family-related absence per year are **paid** if the employee has 3 months service.
- Balance is unpaid (EI Compassionate Care eligible).

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer as soon as possible.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request a document proving the medical or serious grounds.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Quebec is very generous with child-related illness leaves (up to 104 weeks).
- The 2 paid days (shared with sick leave) is a unique benefit.
