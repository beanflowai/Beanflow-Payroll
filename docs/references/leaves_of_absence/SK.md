## Province-Specific Notes
- **Recent Change (Jan 2026)**: Serious Illness leave extended to 27 weeks. Bereavement leave extended to 5 days and expanded to include "like family".
- **Eligibility**: Most leaves in SK require **13 weeks** of employment.
- SK is one of the few provinces offering 5 days of *paid* Interpersonal Violence leave.
