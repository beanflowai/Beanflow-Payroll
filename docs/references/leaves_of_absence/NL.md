- **Recent Change (2026)**: Proposed extension of sick/injury leave to 27 weeks to match federal standards.
- **Paid Days**: NL offers 1 paid bereavement day after 30 days of service.
- **Family Violence**: 3 paid days + 7 unpaid days.
