## Province-Specific Notes
- **Eligibility**: Most leaves in Alberta require **90 days** of employment with the same employer.
- **Critical Illness**: Alberta updated its "Long-term Illness and Injury Leave" to 27 weeks effective Jan 1, 2026.
- **Bereavement**: Covers pregnancy loss.
