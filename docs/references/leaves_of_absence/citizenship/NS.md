## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Employee attending their own citizenship ceremony.

### Entitlement
- 1 day of unpaid, job-protected leave.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least **14 days' written notice** from the employee.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- NS has a specific 14-day notice requirement for citizenship leave.
