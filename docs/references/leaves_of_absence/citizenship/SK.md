## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for more than **13 consecutive weeks**.
- Employee who is attending their own citizenship ceremony.

### Entitlement
- 1 day of unpaid, job-protected leave.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer as soon as possible.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof of the event.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 1 full day is more generous than BC (1/2 day).
