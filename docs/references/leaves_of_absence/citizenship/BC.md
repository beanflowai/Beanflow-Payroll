## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to an employee who is attending their own citizenship ceremony on a working day.

### Entitlement
- Up to a half-day (1/2 day) of unpaid leave.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Employee must give the employer notice as soon as possible.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof of the citizenship ceremony (e.g., invitation).

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Many provinces offer 1 full day (e.g., SK, MB).
- Some provinces do not have a specific legislated citizenship leave.
