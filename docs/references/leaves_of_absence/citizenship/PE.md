## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have at least **3 months** of continuous service.
- Employee who is attending their own citizenship ceremony.

### Entitlement
- 1 day of unpaid, job-protected leave.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer as soon as practicable.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- PEI's citizenship leave matches the 1-day standard in SK/MB/NB.
