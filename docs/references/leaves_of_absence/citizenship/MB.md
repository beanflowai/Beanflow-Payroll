## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for at least **30 days**.
- Attending own citizenship ceremony.

### Entitlement
- Up to 4 hours or a full day depending on the ceremony timing (standardized to 1 day for documentation).

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must notify employer as soon as possible.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof of the ceremony.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
