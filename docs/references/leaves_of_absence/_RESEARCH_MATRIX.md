## P0 Research Summary (Family Violence, Bereavement, Critical Illness)

### BC
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid, 15 Weeks. Immediate.
- **BR**: 3 Unpaid per instance. Immediate. 104 wks child death.
- **CI**: 27 wks unpaid (serious illness - self). 36 wks (child care). 16 wks (adult care).

### AB
- **FV**: 10 days Unpaid. 90-day service.
- **BR**: 3 days Unpaid. 90-day service. Includes pregnancy loss.
- **CI**: 27 wks unpaid (self). 36 wks (child). 27 wks (family care). 90-day service.

### SK
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid + 16 weeks continuous.
- **BR**: 5 days Unpaid (within 6 months of death).
- **CI**: 27 wks unpaid (serious illness).

### MB
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid + 17 weeks. 90-day service.
- **BR**: 5 days Unpaid. 30-day service.
- **CI**: 27 wks (self), 37 wks (child), 17 wks (adult). 90-day service (except child: 30-day).

### ON
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid + 15 weeks. 13-week service.
- **BR**: 2 days Unpaid. Immediate.
- **CI**: 27 wks (self), 37 wks (child), 17 wks (adult). 13-week service.

### QC
- **FV**: 26 weeks Unpaid. 3-month service.
- **BR**: 5 days (2 Paid for close family). 3-month service for pay.
- **CI**: 26-27 weeks Unpaid (care for relative). 3-month service.

### NB
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid + 16 weeks. 90-day service.
- **BR**: 5 days Unpaid. Immediate.
- **CI**: 37 wks (child), 16 wks (adult), 28 wks (compassionate).

### NS
- **FV**: 3 Paid, 7 Unpaid + 15 weeks. Immediate.
- **BR**: 3 days Unpaid. Immediate.
- **CI**: 37 wks (child), 16 wks (adult), 28 wks (compassionate).

### PE
- **FV**: 3 Paid, 7 Unpaid. 3-month service.
- **BR**: 1 Paid, 2 Unpaid. Immediate. Covers pregnancy loss.
- **CI**: 37 wks (child), 28 wks (compassionate).

### NL
- **FV**: 3 Paid, 7 Unpaid. 30-day service.
- **BR**: 1 Paid, 2 Unpaid (30-day service).
- **CI**: 27 wks (self), 37 wks (child), 17 wks (adult).

### YT
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid + 15 weeks. 3-month service.
- **BR**: 1 week Unpaid.
- **CI**: 37 wks (child), 17 wks (adult), 28 wks (compassionate).

### NT
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid + 15 weeks.
- **BR**: 3-5 days Unpaid (Family Responsibility).
- **CI**: 37 wks (child), 17 wks (adult), 27 wks (compassionate).

### NU
- **FV**: 10 days Unpaid.
- **BR**: 3-5 days Unpaid (Family Responsibility).
- **CI**: 37 wks (child), 17 wks (adult), 8 wks (compassionate).

### Federal
- **FV**: 5 Paid, 5 Unpaid. 3-month service.
- **BR**: 10 days (3 Paid). 3-month service for pay.
- **CI**: 27 wks (self/medical), 37 wks (child), 17 wks (adult).
