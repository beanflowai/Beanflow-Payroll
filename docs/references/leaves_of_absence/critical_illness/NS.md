## Detailed Rules

### 1. Critically Ill Child Leave
- **Duration**: Up to 37 weeks within a 52-week period.
- **Eligibility**: 3 months service.

### 2. Critically Ill Adult Leave
- **Duration**: Up to 16 weeks within a 52-week period.
- **Eligibility**: 3 months service.

### 3. Compassionate Care Leave
- **Duration**: Up to 28 weeks within a 52-week period.
- **Eligibility**: 3 months service.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Aligns with federal standards for duration (37/16/28 weeks), with a 3-month service requirement which is standard for the Atlantic provinces.
