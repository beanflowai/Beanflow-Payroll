## Detailed Rules

### 1. Serious Illness or Injury Leave (FOR SELF)
**Effective June 19, 2025**, Ontario introduced a **27-week unpaid leave** for employees unable to work due to a serious medical condition.
- **Eligibility**: 13 weeks of service.
- **Requirement**: Certificate from a qualified health practitioner.

### 2. Critical Illness Leave (TO CARE FOR OTHERS)
- **Eligibility**: 6 months of service.
- **Duration (Child <18)**: Up to 37 weeks.
- **Duration (Adult Family Member)**: Up to 17 weeks.
- **Compassionate Care**: Up to 28 weeks.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Ontario's recent addition of the 27-week self-illness leave brings it into alignment with federal and BC/AB standards. The 6-month eligibility for caring for others is slightly higher than the 3-month/90-day requirement in western provinces.
