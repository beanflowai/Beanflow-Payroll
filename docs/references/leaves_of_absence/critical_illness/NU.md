## Detailed Rules

### 1. Critically Ill Child/Adult Leave
- **Duration**: 37 weeks (child) / 17 weeks (adult).
- **Eligibility**: Immediate.

### 2. Compassionate Care Leave
- **Duration**: 8 weeks (statutory minimum in LSA), but usually aligns with the 27-week EI standard for practical purposes.
- **Eligibility**: Immediate.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Nunavut's statutory minimum for compassionate care (8 weeks) is the lowest in Canada, but most employers follow federal EI standards (27 weeks).
