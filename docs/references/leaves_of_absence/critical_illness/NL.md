## Detailed Rules

### 1. Critically Ill Child/Adult Leave
- **Duration**: 37 weeks (child) / 17 weeks (adult).
- **Eligibility**: 30 days service.

### 2. Serious Illness Leave (PROPOSED 2026)
- **Duration**: Proposed 27 weeks to match federal EI.
- **Eligibility**: 30 days service.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
NL's 30-day eligibility is lower than the typical 90-day/3-month requirement in other provinces, making it one of the more accessible critical illness leaves.
