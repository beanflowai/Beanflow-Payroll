## Detailed Rules

### 1. Long-Term Leave for Serious Injury or Illness (FOR SELF)
- **Duration**: Up to 27 weeks.
- **Eligibility**: 90 days of employment.

### 2. Critical Illness Leave (TO CARE FOR OTHERS)
- **Duration (Child <18)**: Up to 37 weeks. Eligibility: 30 days.
- **Duration (Adult Family Member)**: Up to 17 weeks. Eligibility: 90 days.
- **Compassionate Care**: Up to 28 weeks.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Manitoba's 37-week period for child care is one week longer than the typical 36 weeks found in other provinces, aligning with federal standards.
