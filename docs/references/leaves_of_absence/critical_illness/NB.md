## Detailed Rules

### 1. Critically Ill Child Leave
- **Duration**: Up to 37 weeks within a 52-week period.
- **Eligibility**: Immediate.

### 2. Critically Ill Adult Leave
- **Duration**: Up to 16 weeks within a 52-week period.
- **Eligibility**: Immediate.

### 3. Compassionate Care Leave
- **Duration**: Up to 28 weeks within a 52-week period (care for person with risk of death).
- **Eligibility**: Immediate.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
New Brunswick is notable for having **immediate eligibility** for these leaves, whereas many other provinces (AB, MB) require 90 days.
