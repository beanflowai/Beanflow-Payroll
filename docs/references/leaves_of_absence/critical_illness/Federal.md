## Detailed Rules

### 1. Medical Leave (FOR SELF)
- **Duration**: Up to 27 weeks.
- **Paid Days**: Accrual of 1 day per month (after 30 days) up to 10 days; 3 days paid after 3 months service.

### 2. Critical Illness Leave (TO CARE FOR OTHERS)
- **Duration (Child <18)**: 37 weeks.
- **Duration (Adult)**: 17 weeks.
- **Compassionate care**: 28 weeks.
- **Eligibility**: Immediate.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Federal standards define the "27-week" sickness benefit and "37-week" child care norms followed by most provinces.
