## Detailed Rules

### 1. Serious Illness or Injury Leave (FOR SELF)
**Effective January 1, 2026**, the duration of serious illness leave increased to **27 weeks** (from 12).
- **Eligibility**: 13 weeks of employment.
- **Requirement**: Medical cert if requested.

### 2. Critical Illness Leave (TO CARE FOR OTHERS)
- **Eligibility**: 13 weeks of employment.
- **Duration (Child)**: Up to 37 weeks.
- **Duration (Adult)**: Up to 17 weeks.
- **Requirement**: Medical certificate.

### 3. Payment Calculation
Unpaid.

## Comparison with Federal/Other Provinces
Saskatchewan's 13-week eligibility is the highest in Western Canada (vs AB's 90 days). The 2026 update to 27 weeks for self-illness matches federal standards.
