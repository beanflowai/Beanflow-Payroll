## Province-Specific Notes
- **Family Violence**: One of the most generous, offering 5 paid days + 5 unpaid days + 17 weeks.
- **Bereavement**: Entitlement is 5 days (vs 3 in BC/AB).
- **Eligibility**: Varies significantly in MB (30 days for bereavement/critically ill child, 7 months for maternity).
