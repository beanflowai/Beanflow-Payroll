## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum length of service specified in the Act for Jury Duty (considered immediate).
- Applies to employees summoned for jury selection or jury duty.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the entire duration of the requirement.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: Jurors in SK receive $110 per day plus mileage expenses.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of jury summons to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- One of many provinces where jury duty is unpaid by the employer.
