## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to employees summoned for jury duty or required to attend court as a witness.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the duration of the court requirement.
- Section 31.5 of the Employment Standards Act specifically protects employees.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: Jurors in NT receive $80 per day plus expenses.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of summons to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Typical unpaid court leave.
