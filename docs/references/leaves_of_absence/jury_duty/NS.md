## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to employees summoned for jury service or required to attend court as a witness.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the duration of the court requirement.
- Section 60(2) specifically protects employees from dismissal or penalty.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: Jurors in Nova Scotia receive $40 per day plus expenses.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of summons to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
