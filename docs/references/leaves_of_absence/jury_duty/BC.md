## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to any employee summoned or selected for jury service or required to attend court as a witness.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the entire duration of the court requirement.
- Leave is considered continuous service for seniority and vacation entitlement.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: The BC government pays jurors a small daily rate after the 10th day ($20/day for days 2-10, $60/day for days 11-49, etc.). Employers are not required to top this up but may choose to.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of summons/subpoena to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Most provinces specify unpaid leave for jury duty.
- **Newfoundland and Labrador** is the only province that requires employers to pay for jury duty.
