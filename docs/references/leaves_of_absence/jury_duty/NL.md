## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to employees summoned for jury duty or required to attend court as a witness.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the duration of the court requirement.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: **Must pay the employee** the same wages and benefits they would have received if they had worked.
- NL is the **only province** in Canada that mandates employer payment for jury duty.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of summons to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- **Unique**: NL is the only jurisdiction where the employer must pay full wages during jury duty.
