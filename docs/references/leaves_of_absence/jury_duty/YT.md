## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to employees summoned for jury duty.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the duration of the court requirement.
- Employers are prohibited from dismissing or penalizing an employee for jury duty.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: Jurors in Yukon receive $80 per day plus expenses.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of summons to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
