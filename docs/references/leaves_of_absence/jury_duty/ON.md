## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to employees summoned for jury selection or jury duty.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the duration of the court requirement.
- Section 43 of the Juries Act prohibits employers from dismissing, suspending, or penalizing an employee for jury service.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: Jurors in Ontario receive:
    - Days 1-10: $0
    - Days 11-49: $40/day
    - Day 50+: $100/day

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of jury summons to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Standard unpaid jury duty leave.
