## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- No minimum service requirement.
- Applies to employees summoned or selected for jury duty.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the duration of the court requirement.
- Section 32 of the Jurors Act prohibits employers from dismissing, suspending, or penalizing an employee.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: Jurors in Quebec receive an indemnity (approx. $160 per day plus expenses).

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of summons to employer.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Standard unpaid jury duty.
