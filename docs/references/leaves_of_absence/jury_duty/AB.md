## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **90 days**.
- Applies to employees summoned or selected for jury service.

### Entitlement
- Job-protected leave for the duration of the court requirement.
- Leave is considered continuous service for seniority.

### Payment Calculation
- **Employer Obligation**: Unpaid.
- **Court Payment**: Alberta pay jurors $50 per day for jury service, plus travel expenses.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Provide copy of jury summons to employer as soon as received.
- **Verification**: Employer can request proof of attendance from the clerk of the court.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Most provinces require unpaid leave.
- 90-day eligibility is unique to Alberta (BC and others are immediate).
