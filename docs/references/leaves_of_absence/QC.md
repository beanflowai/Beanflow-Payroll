## Province-Specific Notes
- **LSA (Labor Standards Act)**: Quebec's rules (via CNESST) differ significantly in terminology and structure from other provinces.
- **Paid Days**: Quebec offers 2 paid days for many causes (sickness, bereavement) after 3 months of service.
- **Suicide/Criminal Offense**: Unique long-term leave (104 weeks) for death of a child/spouse by suicide or criminal act.
