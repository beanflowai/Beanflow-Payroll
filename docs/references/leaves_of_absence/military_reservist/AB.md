## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **26 weeks**.
- Applies to employees who are members of the Canadian Forces reserves.

### Entitlement
- Up to 20 days per calendar year for annual training.
- Length of deployment for operations (domestic or international).
- Job-protected leave.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice of the start and end dates.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request a document from the reservist's commanding officer.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 26-week eligibility is consistent with ON and SK.
- BC has no minimum service for reservists.
