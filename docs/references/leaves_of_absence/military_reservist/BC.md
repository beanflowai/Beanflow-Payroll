## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- **No minimum service requirement** (Note: Most other provinces require 6-12 months of service).
- Applies to employees who are members of the Canadian Forces reserves.

### Entitlement
- Unpaid leave for the duration of the reservist's deployment to an operation (domestic or international).
- Includes training activities.
- The leave is considered continuous service for seniority and vacation.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must give at least 4 weeks' written notice of the start and end dates of the leave, unless impossible (e.g., emergency deployment).
- **Documentation**: Employer may request proof of membership in the reserves and the requirement to participate in the operation/training.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- BC is unique in having **no minimum service requirement** for reservists. Most other provinces require 6 or 12 months.
