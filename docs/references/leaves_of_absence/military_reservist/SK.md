## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for more than **13 consecutive weeks**.
- Applies to employees who are members of the Canadian Forces reserves.

### Entitlement
- Unpaid, job-protected leave for the duration of deployment to an operation (domestic/international) or for training.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: 4 weeks' written notice of the start and end dates (unless impossible).
- **Documentation**: Employer can request a certificate from the reservist's commanding officer.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 13-week eligibility is more generous than Alberta (26 weeks).
