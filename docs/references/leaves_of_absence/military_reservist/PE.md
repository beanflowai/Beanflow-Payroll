## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have at least **6 months** of continuous service.
- Applies to members of the Canadian Forces reserves.

### Entitlement
- Unpaid, job-protected leave for the duration of military service.
- Includes training and deployment.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof from commanding officer.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 6-month eligibility matches Ontario (for training) and several other provinces.
