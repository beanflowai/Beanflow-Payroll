## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have at least **3 months** of continuous service.
- Applies to members of the Canadian Forces reserves.

### Entitlement
- Unpaid, job-protected leave for the duration of military service.
- Includes training, deployment, and recovery from injury sustained during service.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice before start.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof from commanding officer.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- Federal 3-month eligibility is shorter than many provinces (e.g., 6 months in ON, 12 months in QC/BC).
