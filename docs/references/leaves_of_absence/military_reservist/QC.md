## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have at least **12 months** of continuous service.
- Applies to members of the Canadian Forces reserves.

### Entitlement
- Unpaid, job-protected leave for the duration of military service.
- Maximum duration of **2 years** total for certain operations.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: At least 4 weeks' written notice (unless impossible).
- **Documentation**: Employer can request a document from the reservist's commanding officer.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 12-month eligibility is longer than Ontario (2 months) but typical for several other provinces.
