## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed by the same employer for at least **7 consecutive months**.
- Applies to members of the Canadian Forces reserves.

### Entitlement
- Unpaid, job-protected leave for the duration of military service.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must give at least 4 weeks' written notice of the start and end dates (unless impossible).
- **Documentation**: Employer can request a certificate from the reservist's commanding officer.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 7-month eligibility matches MB's maternity leave requirement.
