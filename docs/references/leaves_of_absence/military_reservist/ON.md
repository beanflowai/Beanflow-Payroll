## Detailed Rules

### Eligibility
- Must have been employed for at least **2 consecutive months** for deployment.
- Must have been employed for at least **6 consecutive months** to participate in training.

### Entitlement
- Unpaid, job-protected leave for the duration of military service.
- Includes deployment for domestic operations, international operations, and training.

### Payment Calculation
- Unpaid.

### Notice & Documentation
- **Notice**: Must give reasonable written notice of the start and end dates.
- **Documentation**: Employer can request proof of the requirement to participate.

## Comparison with Other Jurisdictions
- 2-month eligibility for deployment is one of the shortest (most are 6-12 months).
