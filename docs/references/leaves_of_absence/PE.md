## Province-Specific Notes
- **Paid Days**: PEI mandates 1 paid sick day and 1 paid bereavement day.
- **Family Violence**: 3 paid days + 7 unpaid days per 12-month period.
- **Bereavement**: Covers pregnancy loss.
