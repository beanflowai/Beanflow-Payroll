## Jurisdiction-Specific Notes
- **Bereavement (Dec 2025 Updates)**: 10 days total (3 paid if 3 months service).
- **Sick Leave**: Accrues 1 day per month up to 10 days; 3 days are paid after 3 months service.
- **Family Violence**: 5 days paid + 5 days unpaid.
